Vivo Y21T with 90Hz display, 5000mAh battery launched in India at Rs 16,490





Highlights

  1. Vivo’s Y21T sports a 6.58 inch Full HD+ IPS LCD display with a screen refresh rate of 90 Hz.
  2. Vivo Y21T price in India has been set at Rs 19,990 for the 4GB RAM with 128GB storage variant.
  3. It features a triple rear camera setup that includes a 50-megapixel primary sensor with an f/1.8 aperture

Vivo Y21T has been launched in India just days after debuting in Indonesia. It is the latest Y-series phone from the company and is positioned in the mid-range segment. Priced at Rs. 16,490, the smartphone offers 4GB+128GB storage and Extended RAM 2.0 that makes use of up to 1GB idle ROM space.Yogendra Sriramula, Director, Brand Strategy, Vivo India, said in a statement, “Vivo's youthful Y-Series is designed for millennials who always have a knack for immersive experience and technology. The Y21T offers best-in-class features providing customers a premium experience.”

The Vivo Y21T has a triple rear camera setup consisting of a 50 megapixel primary camera, 2 megapixel macro lens, and a 2 megapixel depth sensor. For selfies and video calls, the company has provided an 8 megapixel camera sensor on the front. It also includes a number of camera features such as Personalized Portrait Mode, Super HDR, Super Night Mode, AI beautification mode, AI Editor, and more.

The 'Multi Turbo 5.0'feature enhances data connection, system processor speed, and power-saving performance to a new level even after prolonged usage. Connectivity options on the newly announced smartphone include 4G LTE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth v5, GPS/ A-GPS, FM radio, USB Type-C, and a 3.5mm headphone connector
